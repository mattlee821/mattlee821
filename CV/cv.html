Research experience

I'm a genetic epidemiologist using Mendelian randomization, metabolomics, and composite measures to investigate disease development. I have performed genome-wide association studies, conducted a systematic review and meta-analysis of Mendelian randomization studies, and developed a visualisation tool for metabolomics analyses. I've led and managed researchers, taught PhD students, post-docs and clinicians, and written successful grants. In addition, I have experience in molecular and cell biology of tumour microenvironments and have worked closely with wet-lab scientists.
